SIT223-SIT753 - Professional Practice in IT

Unit Content needs

Professional Practice in Information and Technology unit provides a comprehensive exploration of essential skills and knowledge for IT professionals. The course begins with foundational concepts like SFIA, project management, and Agile methodologies, progressively building on these with more advanced topics such as DevOps practices, including build, test, deploy, and monitor stages. Alongside technical skills, the unit emphasises professional development with modules focused on job readiness, ethics, intellectual property, and leadership. By integrating technical skills with team building assignments, this unit helps the enrolled students to excel in the field of IT.

Hurdle Requirement

To be eligible to obtain a pass in this unit, students must meet certain milestones as part of the portfolio.
